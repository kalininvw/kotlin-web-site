Safety precheck builder usage

Goal:
- Convert family manifest into actionable safety tasks before any controlled build.

Run:
  python3 /workspace/scripts/safety_precheck_builder.py \
    --manifest-csv "/workspace/uploads/family_manifest_run/family_manifest.csv" \
    --out-dir "/workspace/uploads/safety_precheck_run"

Outputs:
- safety_precheck_tasks.csv
- controlled_build_queue.csv
- safety_precheck_summary.txt

Interpretation:
- If controlled_build_queue is empty, do NOT start build pipeline.
- Resolve all tasks in safety_precheck_tasks first.
