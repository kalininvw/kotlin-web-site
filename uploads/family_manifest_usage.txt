Family manifest builder usage (safety-gate first)

Goal:
- Merge firmware index + ident priority + stage readiness into a single control table.
- Enforce conservative safety gates before any build/write workflow.

Run:
  python3 /workspace/scripts/family_manifest_builder.py \
    --firmware-index-csv "/workspace/uploads/yadisk_ingest_run/firmware_catalog_index.csv" \
    --ident-priority-csv "/workspace/uploads/ident_run/ident_brand_priority_toyota_haval.csv" \
    --stage-families-csv "/workspace/uploads/stage_core_run/stage_core_families.csv" \
    --out-dir "/workspace/uploads/family_manifest_run"

Outputs:
- family_manifest.csv
- family_manifest_toyota.csv
- family_manifest_haval.csv
- family_manifest_summary.txt
- checksum_capability_registry_v1.json

Safety logic:
- BLOCKED:
  missing stock or modified reference.
- PRECHECK_REQUIRED:
  checksum not project-verified.
- READY_FOR_CONTROLLED_BUILD:
  only when no blockers remain.
